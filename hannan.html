Exploring Pakistan's Northern Areas

Pakistan's Northern Areas are paradise for nature lovers. The Karakoram Range and green valleys offer breathtaking landscapes that amaze every visitor.

Hunza Valley is stunning with crystal-clear waters and snow-capped peaks. Attabad Lake's turquoise water is unforgettable.

Skardu is home to K2 and beautiful Sheosar Lake. The Shangrila Resort is known as "Heaven on Earth."

Naran and Kaghan Valley offer scenic beauty. Lake Saif-ul-Malook at 10,578 feet is surrounded by snow-covered peaks.

Fairy Meadows at the base of Nanga Parbat offers spectacular views. The green meadows and dense forests attract trekkers worldwide.
